  • 🔧 Deep Visual & Interactive Assembly:

    Install motherboards, CPUs, cooler fans, GPUs, power supplies, RAM sticks, and storage drives. Cable them up manually and apply standard thermal paste or upgrade to Premium Liquid Metal 🔥 to reduce load temperatures by -8°C and smash benchmark targets!

    Read incoming client requests to clean malware, diagnose BSODs, upgrade memory, or build custom computers from scratch. Watch out for 🚨 URGENT 24H contracts: they pay double cash and experience but will expire and cancel if you don't deliver them before ending the day!

  • 🛒 Dynamic Parts Shop & Tech News:

    Order components with Standard shipping (1 day delivery) or instant Express shipping. Keep a close eye on the morning's Tech News Flash 📰! Global silicon shortages, memory crises, or Black Friday sales will cause hardware prices to fluctuate dramatically in real-time.

  • 🔄 BargainBin Flipping & Active Negotiations:

    Buy broken or used PCs on the second-hand market, repair their faulty parts, and list them for sale. Negotiate directly with buyers who make custom offers! Accept, refuse, or counter-propose the perfect price to maximize your flipping margins without scaring them away.

  • 🏆 Prestige Showroom & Brand Sponsors:

    Expose your benchmark masterpieces in your private showroom. Your cumulative prestige score unlocks global brand sponsors (ASUS, AMD, NVIDIA, Intel, Corsair, NZXT) granting you permanent shop discounts of up to -20% on all hardware components!
